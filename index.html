A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Rose Briggs!

 I am a game developer from western Massachusetts currently studying interactive media and game development (IMGD) at WPI.

 I am a game systems programmer who specializes in fluid player movement systems.

 The majority of my experience in game development is in programming, but I also have a little experience in 3D art (modeling, rigging, and animation), foley sound effects, and music.
This website was almost entirely made by me using HTML and CSS! The git repository is available here.

No AI was used in the process of creating this website or any of my work.
