A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Rose Briggs!

I am a game developer from western Massachusetts currently studying interactive media and game development at WPI. My main interest in game development is 2D platformers, but I enjoy making just about any kind of game! The majority of my experience in game development is in programming, but I also have a little experience in 3D art (modeling, rigging, and animation), foley sound effects, and music.
This website was made almost entirely by me using HTML and CSS!
